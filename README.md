Min Haddak is a Lebanese online service marketplace designed to help users find trusted local professionals and artisans across Lebanon. The site lets visitors enter their location and choose a profession—such as plumber, electrician, carpenter, mechanic, cleaner, home hairdresser, personal trainer, and more—to search for nearby service providers. It also offers options for clients (service seekers) and pros (artisans), and promotes installation of its app for easier access. 
www.minhaddak.com

In short: Min Haddak connects people in Lebanon with local skilled workers and service providers through a searchable marketplace interface
